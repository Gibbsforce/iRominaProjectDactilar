We are a peruvian fashion proyect founded in 2020.

In this world that runs faster and faster, that does not stop spinning without rest, we like to think that time can slow down by the power of what is made by hand.

We want to leave our mark impregnated with positive emotions, with pieces based on the free spirit, charged with multicolored energy and that contribute to the conservation of the environment through handcrafted practices.

Dactilar is disigned and created by Romina Pozo.
